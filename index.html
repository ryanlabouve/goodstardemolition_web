Our Story

Our team's story began during the 2013 Moore Tornado. We saw entire neighborhoods disappear, and many peoples' lives and homes destroyed. We did what we could—giving out supplies, helping search the rubble for keepsakes, and clearing away debris.

From that day forward, Goodstar Demolition was born. A company that would be trained and equipped to step in the next time disaster strikes. Disaster doesn't stay away too long. From tornadoes to hurricanes to ice storms—we're there.
